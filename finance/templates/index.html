Welcome to FinDjango

FinDjango is sample/test financial consultancy application.
Here you can get advice or give advice. If you want to use the app you have must be login!

  • Click on Get Token and choose a package and go to the payment screen.
  • Enter the card number as 4111 1111 1111 1111. Enter a random number in the other fields.
  • The token will be loaded into your account.
  • Select and buy one of the advices on the homepage.

  • Click on Create Advice.
  • The first currency must gain value against the second currency of the target date.
  • Select the target date.
  • Write the info.
  • Write the analysis that buyers will see.
  • Enter the token amount.
  • And create.
